Market Simulation

A six-week trading simulation where members manage a virtual portfolio, write weekly investment memos, and defend their decisions in front of the cohort.

  • Real market data, virtual capital — no real money at risk
  • Weekly memos: thesis, position, sizing, exit conditions
  • Live cohort review of every trade after the fact
  • Final-week presentations to a panel of staff editors and alumni
