Our Club Our Staff PEB Charter
Our club

A student-led union of curious, collaborative, globally-minded learners.

PEB Union is the Politics, Economics and Business union of Al-Beruni International Boarding School. We are a student-led, non-profit organisation governed by our Charter and operated under the school's guidelines.

Mission

Educate and empower students in politics, economics, and business through peer-supported learning. Organise educational activities, MUN simulations, and maintain a digital platform for podcasts and articles to encourage research and dialogue.

Vision

Build a well-informed, confident, and socially responsible generation of young leaders who actively engage with political, economic, and business issues on a global scale.

Our Core Values · Article II §2.3

The five values that guide our work.

Critical Thinking

Ask questions, think deeply, and use evidence to support ideas.

Collaboration
