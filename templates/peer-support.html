Peer Support

What is Peer Support?

A trained, structured network where students can talk to other students about academic stress, transitions, or simply having a hard week — confidentially, and with clear referral pathways for anything beyond peer scope.

What we offer

  • Drop-in hours every Tuesday and Thursday, 16:00–17:30
  • Help with Global Politics, Economics, and Business subjects
  • IA structuring, source evaluation, and exam preparation strategies
  • All peer supporters complete a structured training programme

What we don't do

We are not therapists, tutors, or counsellors. Anything beyond our scope receives a respectful referral to the appropriate teacher or school counsellor — that's the system working as intended.
