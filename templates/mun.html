Model United Nations

What is MUN?

Model United Nations is PEB's flagship simulation. Members take on the roles of national delegates, debate live policy questions, and draft resolutions under the same procedural rules used at the real United Nations.

What we do

  • Two annual conferences — one internal training conference, one open invitational
  • Beginner-friendly committees alongside advanced General Assembly and Security Council tracks
  • Position-paper workshops, public-speaking coaching, and drafting clinics
  • Travel delegations to regional and international MUN conferences

Leadership

President: Daler Nozimov
Vice-President: Nilufar Umarova

How to join

MUN is open to all PEB members. No prior experience is required — only the willingness to read a position paper and argue in good faith. Applications for external delegations follow Article V, §5.2 of the Charter.
